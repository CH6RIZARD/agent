**What actually happened:**

The plague spread through flea bites from infected rats and person-to-person transmission of pneumonic plague. Wells had nothing to do with it.

**Why the accusations emerged:**

1. **Jews were dying at lower rates** in some areas, which made people suspicious rather than curious about actual causes
2. **The real reasons for lower Jewish mortality:**
   - Jewish religious law required handwashing before meals and prayers
   - Kosher slaughter practices were more hygienic
   - Jewish communities were often segregated in less rat-infested areas
   - Some Jewish dietary and sanitation practices reduced disease transmission

3. **But instead of learning from this**, Christian populations invented a conspiracy theory that Jews were immune *because they'd caused it*

**The persecution:**

- Confessions were extracted under torture (Strasbourg, Basel, Freiburg)
- These "confessions" claimed an international Jewish conspiracy
- Entire communities were burned alive based on tortured false confessions
- Over 200 Jewish communities were destroyed across Europe (1348-1351)
- The Pope explicitly stated Jews weren't responsible, but local authorities ignored this

**Historical pattern:**

This fits a recurring phenomenon - when crisis hits, authorities and populations look for scapegoats rather than addressing actual causes. The minority group doing *better* (due to different practices) gets accused of causing the problem rather than being studied for solutions.

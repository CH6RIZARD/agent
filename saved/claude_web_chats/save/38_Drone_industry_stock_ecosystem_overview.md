## ASSISTANT

 **Market Cap (Market Capitalization)** = the total value of a company's stock

---

## The Simple Formula:

```
Market Cap = Stock Price √ó Total Shares Outstanding
```

---

## Example:

If **UMAC** trades at **$19.44** and has **36.8 million shares** out there:

> $19.44 √ó 36,800,000 = **~$716 million market cap**

That means if you wanted to buy the *entire company* at today's price, it would cost ~$716 million.
